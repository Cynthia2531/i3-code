What we are trying to achieve

We are helping two different problems here in Perth. We are helping get food from cafes and restaurants that would be otherwise wasted because so much food is wasted in the world, but even little bits like what we're doing will make a difference! Another issue is the amount of homeless people around nowadays, they struggle to find food constantly and we give them the leftover foods that would be thrown out anyways. We are trying to achieve less food waste and less homelessness in Perth.

How to help

You can help not only the cafes and restaurants of Perth but also the homeless by volunteering your time to pick up foods from different restaurants and cafes (we supply this information). If you find you dont have a lot of time on your hands you can also donate some money on our website; this money goes towards main things to help keep our company running.
